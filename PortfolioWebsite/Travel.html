Houston:

For the FRC world championships in 2019, me and my robotics team went to Houston, Texas to compete against teams from all over the world. Houston is a beautiful city, and I would love to visit it again.

Cape Canaveral:

On the 50th anniversary of Apollo 11's landing on the moon, my family and I went to the Kennedy Space Center in Cape Canaveral, Florida where they launched all of the Apollo missions. I even got to see LC-39 Pad A, which is where Apollo 11 launched from for its legendary mission into space.
